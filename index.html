Created by Smart Design Studio, Connor is distinctive and individual. Deep slots cut into the profile of the building, bring natural light and air to the centre of the structure and, in turn, break it into a series of independent blocks. A sculptural form to the building suggests it’s suspended from the sky.

Considered, refined and elegant Connor is a unique apartment complex that is both cornerstone to the internationally awarded Central Park development, and a southern gateway to the city.
